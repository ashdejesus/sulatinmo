The Story Behind Women's Month

Women's Month celebrates the achievements of women throughout history and recognizes their struggles for equality. It began as a local event in the early 1900s and has since grown into a worldwide movement.

Key Moments in Women's History

  • 1908 - Women's Day protests for better working conditions in New York.
  • 1911 - First International Women's Day celebrated.
  • 1987 - Women's History Month officially declared in the U.S.
  • Present - Celebrations continue worldwide, highlighting women's achievements.
