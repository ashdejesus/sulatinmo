Government Efforts to Promote Baybayin

In recent years, several bills have been proposed in the Philippine Congress advocating for the inclusion of Baybayin in official government communication and public signage. These include:

  • House Bill No. 1022 – National Writing System Act, mandating Baybayin in government signage, labels, and documents.
  • Senate Bill No. 1899 – Baybayin Act of 2013, proposing official integration in government logos.
  • Senate Bill No. 2086 – Promotion of Baybayin for cultural preservation and education.
  • House Bill No. 4155 – Preservation of indigenous writing systems, particularly Baybayin.

These legislative efforts reflect a growing institutional recognition of Baybayin as both a cultural treasure and a living part of modern Filipino identity.
